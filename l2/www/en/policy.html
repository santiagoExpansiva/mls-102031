Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Collab Engine Inc. ("Collab", "we", "our", or "us") operates the Collab Engine available at https://www.collab.codes . This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the Collab Engine.

When you sign in using a third-party authentication provider such as Google, GitHub, or GitLab, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile avatar image

Note: We do not collect passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by the external provider.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to the Collab Engine
  • Identify you within the system
  • Communicate important service-related information
  • Improve the platform and user experience
  • Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Email Privacy

Your email address is treated as confidential and is not publicly displayed to other users. We use your email only for account identification, service notifications, and security-related communications.

4. Authentication Providers

Authentication is performed through trusted third-party providers (Google, GitHub, GitLab). We receive only basic profile information and do not access your passwords or external account content.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, including secure servers, encrypted communications (HTTPS), and access controls. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or to protect the security and integrity of the platform.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data.

8. Children's Privacy

Collab is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
