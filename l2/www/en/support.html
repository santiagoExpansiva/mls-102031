Common Questions

How do I export my generated code?

Once the mission is complete, you can download the full repository via the CLI or the Alpha Dashboard.

Can I use my own AWS infrastructure?

Yes. Collab is designed for sovereignty. You can deploy the generated containers directly to your own VPC.

Is the AI trained on my data?

No. We use private models. Your business logic and data patterns are never used to train global models.
