FAQ

Common Questions

How do I access my generated code?

Once the mission is complete, Collab Engine automatically pushes the entire codebase to your private GitHub or GitLab repository. You own the code 100%, with full commit history and no vendor lock-in.

Where is my application hosted?

You choose the best fit for your stage. By default, Collab.codes provides a high-performance managed environment where you can develop and go live instantly. However, we believe in Code Sovereignty: at any moment, you can opt to host the system on your own infrastructure (AWS, Private Cloud, etc.). It’s your software, and you decide where it lives.

Is the AI trained on my data?

No. We use private models. Your business logic and data patterns are never used to train global models.
